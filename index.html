How You Can Help

Donation bins have been set up around campus in almost all of the residence halls. You can simply drop your used textbooks into these bins and we will take care of the rest. The money generated from these books will be donated to the Literacy Network of Greater Cincinnati.

If you don't have any textbooks but still want to help, you can donate to our cause below.

If you have no books or are tight on cash, we understand. You can still make a difference by spreading the word. The more people that hear about our mission, the greater the impact we can make to help those who are illiterate.
